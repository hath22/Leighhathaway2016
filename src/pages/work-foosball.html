Foosball
Victoria

Foosball Victoria is a not-for-profit organisation managing competitive foosball (table soccer) clubs and tournaments across Victoria.

My Involvement

As a key member of the Foosball Victoria organisation, I am responsible for all aspects of design and development. This has extended to:

  • Design of logo and branding
  • Web site creation and management
  • Design of business cards, posters, banners, flyers, t-shirts and other marketing materials
  • Creation of logo and branding for the Annual State Open Tournament

Branding

The Foosball Victoria colours are based upon the traditional sporting colours of Victoria. The logo was designed to be versatile and effective on a range of mediums, including print, screen and fabric.

The Website

I designed and built the Foosball Victoria website in 2014 using HTML and CSS and finally implementing it into Wordpress.

Marketing material

For major annual tournaments I create a range of printed marketing material for promotion.
