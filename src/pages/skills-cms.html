Content Management Systems

Developing sites with longevity involves creating a solid authoring experience in the content management system that enables content authors to maintain the appearance of the site without development skills.

Whilst I've had some exposure to Sharepoint and Drupal, the below two CMS's would be the two with which I'm most familiar.

Squiz Matrix

I've been using Squiz Matrix for the past five years and would consider myself an advanced user confident in training others in its use.

Matrix is incredibly powerful with great tools for making complex websites. Its major downfall though is the authoring experience which can be very clunky, therefore I make sure that the sites I build are easy for content authors to maintain.

Wordpress

Many of my personal projects are built onto Wordpress, utilising the vast library of plugins to achieve what the CMS doesn't offer out of the box.
