Of my various disiplines my primary passion is for design. Nothing gives me greater satisfaction than to create something beautiful.

Over the years i've utilisd numerous design tools however the below list of tools are my weapons of choice.

Squiz Matrix

I've been using Squiz Matrix for the past 5 years and would consider myself an advanced user confident in being able to train others in how to use it. Matrix is incredibly powerful with great tools for making complex application like websites. It's major downfall though is the authoring experience which can be very clunky, therefore a large part of my skills within Matrix are in building sites that are easy for content authors to maintain.

Wordpress

Many of my personal projects are built onto Wordpress utilising the vast library of plugins to achieve what the CMS doesn't offer out of the box.
