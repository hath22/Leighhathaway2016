Level Crossing
Removal Authority

The Level Crossing Removal Authority are a state government initiative tasked with overseeing the removal of 50 dangerous and congested level crossings across Melbourne over the next eight years.

The project scope was to provide designs for a complete responsive website that their internal web team could cut and implement. The primary goal of the site was to promote the initiative and provide works information about each of the crossings.

Design

My involvement with this project was limited to the visual design stage where I created polished responsive designs and a UI pattern library in Adobe Photoshop.

The existing brand only included the logo so the website theme was developed from, and extended upon, the logo.

Below you can see the home page at two of the four breakpoints we designed for. Also the mobile view includes a design with the menu open.

The main design challenge was to cater for the 50 level crossings, each with varying amounts and format of data. To accomodate for all these I opted to use a secondary navigation and clearly defined breaks in the content.
