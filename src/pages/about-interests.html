Interests &
Hobbies

I find having interests outside of work very important as a source of inspiration for design work and of solutions to work problems whilst undertaking another activity.

My interests include:

Foosball

When I arrived in Melbourne, there were no formal foosball (table soccer) meet-ups so I created a club in St Kilda. This quickly escalated into running the state-wide organisation, overseeing several clubs and national tournaments. As a player, my highest rank has been #2 in Australia and #134 in the world.

Design

Design is more than work for me. I passionately follow a number of design blogs and look for any opportunities to practise new techniques.

AFL

I enjoy following the North Melbourne AFL team and during the summer I partake in an evening AFL 7's league. In 2015 I represented Wales in the AFL World Cup and played in the wins over the Netherlands and Ethiopia.

Tabletop gaming

I regularly play tabletop boardgames, preferring the social and tactile nature of modern board games to computer gaming.
