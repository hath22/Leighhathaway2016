Melbourne
Ipsum

Melbourne Ipsum was a personal project to give something back to the web design community and to honour two of my passions: design and Melbourne.

Design

The design aimed to highlight some of the keywords from the output text with illustrations and a variety of text styles.

See the below design of the landing page:

Development

The site was built using HTML, CSS and jQuery. I opted not to use CMS as it was unnecessary for a small single page application.

The two biggest challenges were to randomise the string of key words and to assemble a large enough pool of keywords to reduce duplication. See the below example of the output:

North melbourne shinboners middle-aged lycra clad cyclists, bourke st mall the australian open. Rod Laver cold drip coffee, hu tong dumplings chaddie moomba.

Since launching in 2014 over 7,500 designers have enjoyed the benefits of random Melbourne text. See the live site at http://melbourneipsum.com.au
